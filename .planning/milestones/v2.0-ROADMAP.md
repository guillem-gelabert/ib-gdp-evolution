## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Ship Act II in local-proxy mode when the raw ETL inputs are absent, rather than pretending the full upstream pipeline exists.
- Keep Act I and Act II data assets separate so the shipped first-act series remains stable.
- Use a dedicated Act II chart component with shared editorial helpers instead of overloading the existing Act I component.
- Make the axis transition scroll-driven so the EU-15 baseline flattening becomes part of the narrative beat.

**Issues Resolved:**

- The Act I chart now supports hidden points, nearest-x hover, line reveal animation, and the editorial endcap treatment.
- The repo now has a generated comparison-data path for Act II and a shipped multi-line scrollytelling scene.
- Static generation succeeded with the expanded two-act experience.

**Issues Deferred:**

- Browser-based UAT for the full Act I + Act II reading and animation experience remains manual.
- The fully sourced Eurostat/INE ETL path is still deferred until the raw inputs are present in the workspace.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- Local-proxy comparison data stands in for the full planned ETL.
- GSD CLI milestone parsing in this repo still trails the artifact reality and should be revisited in a future milestone.
